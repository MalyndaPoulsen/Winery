Our Story

Plumb Cellars was created by a group of friends that share a love and passion for good wine. In the fall of 2006, the Plumb Cellars' partners, created the first vintage of our flagship blend Damn Straight red wine. As we planted our first vineyard our cofounder, a builder by trade, insisted that all the rows be straight and all the vineyard stakes plumb.
